Online service registration

Registering online service

To register for online shopping, you must log into the server play.mctnet.tk on Minecraft PC. And type the following command in Games Server to set a online shopping password for your account:

/shop online [password] [confirmPassword]

You are required to set a 8-letter password with at least one capital letter, one number character, one non-alphanumeric character. For example:

/shop online Minecr@ft123 Minecr@ft123

You can test the functionality by purchasing an item on this website.

Disabling online service

Online shopping service can be disabled and unregistered anytime when you are logged into the server play.mctnet.tk on Minecraft PC. Type the following command in Games Server:

/shop disableonline

Changing password

Changing password is the same as how you register the online service. If you forget your password, DO NOT ATTEMPT TO FIND IT BY ENTERING TO THE LOGIN PAGE. This might block your IP. You SHOULD change the password in-game instead.

Experimental

The mobShop system is currently still experimental and may experience errors on transactions. If you believe there are problems, you are advised to contact us. We would be happy to help you.

WARNING: For your safety and reduce server traffic, if the user has attempted to enter the online shopping password wrong or creating connections too fast (faster than 1 second) for more than 10 times, the internet address (IP) will be blocked for 5 minutes (300 seconds). Continuously may lead to permanent blocking. To unblock a ban on this matter, please contact us directly.
